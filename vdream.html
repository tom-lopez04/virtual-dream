What is my Virtual Dream?

I've spent so long trying to come up with something creative and thoughtful to say regarding my tastes, inspirations, hopes and dreams.
In reality... I'm just someone trying to get by. I've stared at my screen for so long that I can't help but feel that I am becoming almost... digital. A set of pixels, 1s and 0s, bits and bytes, you get the idea.
While creating this site, I've begun to reminisce on games and other media from my childhood. I've also started to think more about my future.

I hope you like my Virtual Dream.
